About Me

Bonnie Faucett is a software founder and owner of three companies helping estate planning attorneys and small business owners solve their most frustrating problems.

As a practicing litigator then estate planning attorney for 15 years, Bonnie discovered her true passion in helping colleagues simplify their systems and processes in a way that wasn't obvious before.

To date Bonnie has built and sold two companies, became licensed as an attorney in three states, helps 213 attorneys save time with better systems, runs a software company serving estate planning attorneys, leads a team who has drafted 1,307 estate plans, and raises seven children with her Army husband.
